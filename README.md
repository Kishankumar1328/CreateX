# CreateX - AI-Powered Content Generation Platform

CreateX is a comprehensive suite of AI tools designed for content creators. Built with Flask, it provides a variety of features to streamline your content creation process.

## üöÄ Features
- **Audio To Text**: Convert audio files into accurate text transcripts.
- **Video To Text**: Extract text from video content.
- **Grammar Check**: Polish your content with advanced grammar and style checks.
- **Script To Title**: Transform your scripts into catchy, SEO-friendly titles.
- **Script To Description**: Automatically generate engaging descriptions for your content.
- **Title To Thumbnail**: Create stunning thumbnails based on your content titles.
- **Text To Speech**: Convert your scripts into natural-sounding voiceovers.
- **Video To Audio**: Easily extract audio from your video files.
- **YouTube Summarizer**: Get quick insights from any YouTube video.
- **Translator**: Multilingual support for global reach.
- **Image Resizer**: Quick tools for visual asset management.
